# Growth Roadmap & Vision

## The Big Picture

AI Ready Bangladesh exists to ensure that Bangladeshis are not left behind in the Intelligence Age. The business grows in concentric circles:

```
Circle 1: EDUCATE (Books, Courses) — current focus
Circle 2: COMMUNITY (AI Builders Program subscription)
Circle 3: BUILD (AI Tools, SaaS products)
Circle 4: INVEST (100 AI Entrepreneurs program)
Circle 5: PHYSICAL (Seminars, Consultancy, Events)
```

Each circle funds and enables the next.

## Phase 2: Community (2026)

### AI Builders Program (Subscription)

**Concept:** A premium monthly membership for serious AI learners and builders.

**What members get:**
- Regular foundation-to-cutting-edge knowledge updates
- Implementation examples (real-world, Bangladesh-relevant)
- Private community group
- Bi-weekly live Q&A with Adnan
- Early access to new courses and content

**Pricing:** ~৳1,500/month

**Why this matters:**
- Recurring revenue (predictable, scalable)
- Community = network effects = organic growth
- Identifies the most committed members for Phase 4 (entrepreneurs)

### Launch Requirements
- Minimum 1,000 book + course buyers as warm audience
- Community platform decided (Facebook Group? Discord? Custom?)
- Content pipeline for regular updates
- Live Q&A infrastructure

## Phase 4: Invest (2027+)

### 100 AI Entrepreneurs Program

**Vision:** Grow 100 Bangladeshi solopreneurs to $1M/month global revenue through AI-powered apps and SaaS.

**Model:**
- AI Ready Bangladesh guides and invests in promising builders
- Solopreneurs build their own AI products (apps, SaaS, tools)
- Full-stack built by the solopreneur, powered by AI coding tools
- AI Ready provides mentorship, community, and possibly capital

**The Khushu App (ihhya.com) is the prototype** — Adnan's own solopreneur product proving the model works.

**Success metric:** If Adnan can build and scale Khushu as a solopreneur, the model is validated for others.

## Strategic Principles

1. **Don't launch too many things at once.** Adnan's biggest risk is context-switching. Focus on one launch at a time.
2. **Each product must be profitable on its own.** No loss leaders hoping for future upsell.
3. **Community before SaaS.** Build the audience first, then build what they need.
4. **Bangladesh first, global second.** Establish dominance locally before expanding.
5. **Automate operations to free Adnan's time for content and strategy.** This is the entire purpose of AI agent automation.
6. **Revenue from books/courses funds everything.** Protect the cash cow.
