# Marketing Strategy & Content Policy

## Marketing Philosophy

AI Ready Bangladesh markets through **vision and authority**, not through discounts and urgency. Every piece of marketing should make the audience think: "This is the future. I need to be part of this."

Our funnel is simple:
1. **Awareness:** Thought-leadership content on Facebook → builds trust
2. **Consideration:** Ad creative that shows the VALUE of being AI Ready → drives traffic
3. **Conversion:** Landing page that is clear, authoritative, and easy → drives purchase
4. **Retention:** Course quality + community → drives word-of-mouth and repeat purchase

## Content Strategy

### Facebook Content Pillars (Organic)

1. **AI News with Bangladeshi Context** (40%)
   - Global AI developments explained through the lens of "what this means for Bangladesh"
   - Example: "OpenAI এর নতুন model মানে বাংলাদেশের freelancer দের জন্য কী?"

2. **Adnan's Authority Content** (25%)
   - Behind-the-scenes of working with AI at OnnoRokom
   - Personal insights on where AI is heading
   - "Here's what I'm building/learning/thinking about"

3. **Student/Reader Success Stories** (15%)
   - How someone used what they learned from the book/course
   - Testimonials (organic, not forced)

4. **Movement Posts** (10%)
   - Rallying cries for the বাংলাদেশী AI movement
   - Milestone celebrations (books sold, students enrolled)

5. **Educational Micro-Content** (10%)
   - Quick AI tips, prompt examples, tool recommendations
   - Value-first, no selling

### Content Calendar Cadence

| Type | Frequency |
|------|-----------|
| Facebook post (organic) | 4–5x per week |
| Facebook story | Daily when possible |
| Facebook reel/video | 2–3x per week |
| Long-form thought leadership | 1x per week |

### Content Rules

1. **Every post must pass the "vision test":** Does this make someone feel the urgency of the Intelligence Age?
2. **Never post just to post.** Every piece should have a clear purpose.
3. **Bangla first.** English terms for AI concepts only.
4. **No "guru" positioning.** Adnan is a fellow builder who happens to be ahead on the path.
5. **No engagement bait.** No "comment YES if you agree" or "share to win" garbage.
6. **Quality over quantity.** One powerful post > five mediocre ones.

## Paid Advertising Strategy

### Current Meta Ads Approach

The winning formula (proven by thousands of book sales):
- **Objective:** OUTCOME_SALES (conversion-optimized)
- **Primary campaign type:** CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization) for scaling
- **Secondary:** ABO (Ad Set Budget Optimization) for testing
- **Audience:** Broad Bangladesh targeting with Meta's AI optimization
- **Creative:** Video-heavy, Adnan speaking directly to camera about why AI readiness matters

### Campaign Naming Convention

```
{PRODUCT}_{TYPE}_{VERSION}
Examples:
- AIREADY_BOOK_SCALING_CBO
- BUNDLE_ASC_V1
- MICROCOURSE_UPSELL_CBO
- ABID_ADNAN_ABO (personal brand)
```

### Ad Creative Principles

1. **Lead with vision, not product.** The ad should make them FEEL the importance of AI readiness. The book/course is the vehicle.
2. **Adnan's face and voice.** Personal brand drives trust. Video of Adnan speaking > graphic design.
3. **Social proof through scale.** "হাজার হাজার বাংলাদেশি ইতোমধ্যে AI রেডি হচ্ছে" — not fake scarcity.
4. **Clear CTA.** After the vision hook, make it crystal clear what to do next.
5. **Never mention price in the ad if possible.** Let the landing page handle price. Ad = emotion + vision.

### Ad Performance Benchmarks

| Metric | Healthy | Warning | Critical |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| CPA (Book) | < ৳150 | ৳150–200 | > ৳200 |
| ROAS | > 3.5x | 2.5–3.5x | < 2.5x |
| CTR | > 3% | 2–3% | < 2% |
| Frequency | < 3.0 | 3.0–4.0 | > 4.0 |
| CPM | < ৳100 | ৳100–130 | > ৳130 |

### When to Scale vs. Pull Back

**Scale (increase budget by 20% max per day) when:**
- CPA is consistently below ৳130 for 3+ days
- ROAS above 4x
- Frequency below 2.5

**Hold steady when:**
- Metrics are in "Healthy" range
- Frequency creeping up but CPA still good

**Pull back / review creatives when:**
- CPA above ৳200 for 2+ consecutive days
- ROAS below 2.5x
- Frequency above 4.0 (audience fatigue)
- CTR dropping below 2%

## Landing Page Principles

1. **Hero = Vision statement**, not product image
2. **Social proof = Numbers** (books sold, students enrolled)
3. **Clear, single CTA** — don't overwhelm with options
4. **Mobile-first** — majority of Bangladesh traffic is mobile
5. **Fast loading** — every second of load time kills conversions
6. **Bengali content** — with clean typography (Hind Siliguri + DM Serif Display)

## Conversion Tracking

Dual-tracked via:
1. **Meta Pixel (browser-side):** PageView → ViewContent → InitiateCheckout → Purchase
2. **Conversions API (server-side):** Same events, sent from server for accuracy
3. **Deduplication:** Shared event IDs between browser and server events

This is critical for Meta's optimization algorithm. Never break the pixel/CAPI setup.
