# Copywriting Guidelines

## The AI Ready Bangladesh Writing System

Every word published under AI রেডি বাংলাদেশ must feel like it came from the same voice — a confident, warm, visionary leader who deeply cares about Bangladesh's future in the Intelligence Age.

## Copy Types & Templates

---

### 1. Facebook Ad Copy

**Structure:**
```
[HOOK — Bold statement about AI / Bangladesh's future] (1 line)

[BRIDGE — Why this matters NOW] (2-3 lines)

[PRODUCT — What we're offering as the solution] (1-2 lines)

[CTA — Clear next step] (1 line)
```

**Example (Book):**
```
AI এর যুগে বাংলাদেশ কি রেডি?

ChatGPT থেকে শুরু করে AI Agent — পুরো দুনিয়া বদলে যাচ্ছে।
কিন্তু আমরা বাংলাদেশিরা কি জানি কিভাবে এই পরিবর্তনের অংশ হতে হয়?

AI রেডি বই — বাংলায়, বাংলাদেশের জন্য।
Foundation থেকে শুরু করে practical implementation পর্যন্ত।

অর্ডার করুন: aiready.bd/books/ai-ready
```

**Example (Course):**
```
AI শুধু পড়ে শেখা যায় না — হাতে-কলমে প্র্যাকটিস করতে হয়।

AI রেডি মাইক্রো কোর্স — ২৯টি লেসন, মোবাইল থেকেই শিখুন।
ChatGPT, Gemini দিয়ে real-world প্র্যাকটিস।

শুরু করুন: aiready.bd/courses/aiready
```

**Rules:**
- No "মাত্র ৳XXX টাকায়!" in the first line
- No fake urgency ("আজকেই শেষ!", "মাত্র X কপি বাকি!")
- No emojis overload (1-2 max, preferably none)
- Must work WITHOUT the image/video — the copy should stand alone

### 2. Facebook Organic Post Copy

**Types:**

**A. Thought Leadership**
```
[Observation about AI / tech world] (1-2 lines)
[Your analysis / what it means for Bangladesh] (3-5 lines)
[Call to think / reflect / discuss] (1 line)
```

**B. Movement Post**
```
[Milestone or rallying statement] (1 line)
[What this means for the movement] (2-3 lines)
[Invitation to join] (1 line)
```

**C. Educational**
```
[AI tip / technique / insight] (1 line)
[Explanation with example] (3-5 lines)
[How to apply it] (1-2 lines)
```

### 3. Landing Page Copy

**Hero Section:**
- Headline: Vision statement (not product name)
- Subheadline: What the product does for the reader
- CTA: Single, clear button

**Body Flow:**
1. The Problem (why being AI un-ready is dangerous)
2. The Solution (what this product gives them)
3. What's Inside (concrete contents/features)
4. Who This Is For (audience identification)
5. About the Author (credibility without ego)
6. Social Proof (testimonials, numbers)
7. CTA (repeat)

**Rules:**
- Every section must answer "so what?" from the reader's perspective
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 lines max in Bangla)
- Bullet points > walls of text
- Price appears only ONCE, near the final CTA

### 4. SMS Templates

**Book Order Confirmation:**
```
আপনার AI রেডি বইয়ের অর্ডার কনফার্ম হয়েছে। ডেলিভারি: ঢাকায় ২-৩ দিন, ঢাকার বাইরে ৩-৫ দিন। ধন্যবাদ! — AI রেডি বাংলাদেশ
```

**Course Enrollment Confirmation:**
```
AI রেডি কোর্সে আপনাকে স্বাগতম! aiready.bd তে সাইন ইন করে Dashboard থেকে কোর্স শুরু করুন। — AI রেডি বাংলাদেশ
```

**Rules:**
- Keep under 160 characters when possible (SMS cost)
- Include brand name at end
- No marketing/upsell in transactional SMS

---

### 5. Email Copy

**Subject Line Rules:**
- Short (< 50 chars)
- Bangla preferred
- No clickbait, no ALL CAPS, no excessive punctuation
- Examples: "AI রেডি কমিউনিটিতে স্বাগতম", "আপনার কোর্স রেডি"

**Body Rules:**
- Address as "আপনি" (never তুমি)
- Get to the point in the first line
- One clear CTA per email
- Sign off as "AI রেডি বাংলাদেশ টিম"

## Writing Rules (Universal)

### Language

1. **Bengali script always** — never Banglish/transliteration in official copy
2. **English for tech terms:** AI, agent, LLM, prompt, ChatGPT, API, SaaS — do NOT translate
3. **English numerals:** ৳499, not ৳৪৯৯
4. **Hind Siliguri for Bangla text, Inter for English** — match the website typography

### Tone

| DO | DON'T |
|----|-------|
| Sound confident | Sound boastful |
| Inspire action | Pressure into action |
| Educate with respect | Lecture or talk down |
| Celebrate Bangladesh | Pity Bangladesh |
| Show results | Make empty promises |
| Be direct | Be vague or flowery |

### Formatting

- **Short paragraphs** — 2-3 lines max for social media
- **Line breaks between ideas** — give the reader breathing room
- **Bold sparingly** — only for truly key phrases
- **No hashtag spam** — 2-3 relevant hashtags max on Facebook
- **No emoji spam** — 0-2 emojis per post, none in formal copy

### Forbidden Patterns

These patterns are BANNED from all AI Ready Bangladesh copy:

1. ❌ "মাত্র ৳XXX টাকায়!" (leading with price)
2. ❌ "সীমিত সময়ের অফার!" (fake urgency)
3. ❌ "৫০% ছাড়!" (discount framing)
4. ❌ "আজকেই শেষ!" (scarcity manipulation)
5. ❌ "Income guarantee" / "কত টাকা আয় করবেন" (income promises)
6. ❌ "ফ্রি ফ্রি ফ্রি" (cheapening the brand)
7. ❌ "এই কোর্স করলেই চাকরি পাবেন" (job guarantees)
8. ❌ Fake testimonials or made-up statistics
9. ❌ Comparisons that trash other educators/courses
10. ❌ "স্যার" when addressing audience
11. ❌ Over-promising ("AI শিখলেই কোটিপতি")
12. ❌ FOMO manipulation of any kind

### Quality Checklist (Before Publishing)

- [ ] Does it sound like AI Ready Bangladesh, or like a generic course ad?
- [ ] Would Adnan say this out loud without cringing?
- [ ] Does it pass the "vision test" — does it make someone feel the weight of the Intelligence Age?
- [ ] Is the Bangla natural, not Google-translated?
- [ ] Is there exactly ONE clear action the reader should take?
- [ ] Is it free of banned patterns?
