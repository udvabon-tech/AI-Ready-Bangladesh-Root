## Pricing Principles

1. **Books are NEVER discounted.** The printed price is the price. Period.
2. **Courses may have promotional pricing** but never "cheap" — we are a premium thought-leader brand.
3. **Bundles offer value** through combination, not through slashing individual prices.
4. **Price points:** Books at ~৳499 range. Micro-courses at ~৳790 range. Subscription at ~৳1500/month.
5. **No flash sales, no urgency scams, no "limited time" pressure.** The value speaks for itself.
